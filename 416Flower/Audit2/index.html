SEO Issues Priority Matrix
🔧 Technical SEO Issues & Fixes
🔗 Toxic Backlinks (89%)

Issue: Algorithmic penalty risk from 36K+ spammy promo-anchor links

Fix: Disavow toxic links; build new branded/local links

Impact: Stabilizes rankings in 2–4 weeks

📊 Indexation Crisis (74%)

Issue: 74% of pages not indexed by search engines

Fix: Remove noindex tags, fix canonicals, update sitemap

Impact: Indexed pages grow from 26% → 60%+

⚡ Core Web Vitals Failing

Issue: Mobile LCP > 2.5s, CLS shifts affecting UX

Fix: WebP images, lazy loading, minify JS/CSS, CDN

Impact: Better mobile rankings & user experience

🔄 Redirect Issues

Issue: Redirect chains and broken internal links

Fix: Update links to final URLs, remove multi-hop redirects

Impact: Improved crawl efficiency and user experience

📋 Missing Schema Markup

Issue: No structured data for products and local business

Fix: Add Product, LocalBusiness & FAQ schema

Impact: Enhanced search result appearance

📝 On-Page & Content SEO
📄 Thin Category Pages

Issue: Category pages have <100 words of content

Fix: Expand to 150–200 words with keyword-rich content

🎯 Keyword Gaps vs Competitors

Issue: Missing pages for high-volume keywords

Fix: Create pages for "fleuriste," "sympathy flowers Toronto," etc.

🏷️ Weak Meta Tags

Issue: Generic meta titles and descriptions

Fix: Rewrite with USP + location + emotion

📝 Blog Underperforming

Issue: Blog not driving organic traffic

Fix: Update old posts, publish 2/month with targeted keywords
