🔑 Top 7 Strategic Priorities for Q4

1

🎁 Year-End Matching Gift Campaign

Partner with corporations or major donors to match contributions during Q4, especially around Giving Tuesday (Dec 2, 2025). Matching doubles perceived donor impact and drives urgency.

2

📱 Frictionless Mobile Donations (Apple Pay & Google Pay)

Enable Apple Pay & Google Pay directly on donation forms for 1-tap checkout. These methods increase completion rates by removing friction.

  • Apple Pay requires NFCR's Candid Seal of Transparency + valid merchant account
  • Google Pay integrates via Stripe or similar
  • Best for one-time gifts; recurring donations can use FundraiseUp
3

🎯 Personalized Donor Segmentation with HubSpot

Deploy persona-based messaging:

  • Past donors: Impact updates + legacy stories
  • Younger donors: UGC, viral challenges, social-driven appeals
  • HNW donors: Tax benefit framing + recognition campaigns
4

📖 Multi-Channel Storytelling Campaigns

Leverage HubSpot + Google Grant + Social Media to run integrated campaigns.

  • Emotional storytelling: survivor stories, lab breakthroughs
  • Seasonal landing pages: Thanksgiving, Holidays, Year-End Giving
  • Targeted retargeting ads for site visitors in the last 300 days
5

🌟 Viral #CureChallenge Social Campaign

Launch a holiday peer challenge (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts). Donors share "Why I Give to NFCR" and challenge friends. Easy, viral, and low cost—builds community + donor acquisition.

6

🤝 Influencer "Story Swap" Partnerships

Engage 5–10 micro-influencers (cancer survivors, advocates) to share NFCR impact stories on their platforms. Authentic storytelling can multiply reach, especially leading into holidays.

7

🎮 Gamified Peer-to-Peer Holiday Raffle

Launch a digital raffle: each $10 donation = 1 entry. Prizes include research lab tours, branded gear, or donor spotlight features. Powered via Raisely/Angelink. Gamification boosts engagement + repeat giving.
