🧠 Executive Summary

Core Story

Strong lead engine in North Carolina (Botox / Fillers) powered by PMax & Search. Scale capped by compliance pauses, keyword waste, and under-leveraged CRM + local presence. Organic search heavily branded (83%), limiting discovery growth.

Objective: Double qualified leads (615 → 1,230/mo) while dropping CPL below $50 within 6–12 months.

Growth Thesis

  • Activate & Clean: Resolve policy, kill waste (negatives, redundants) = immediate lift.
  • Optimize & Train: CRM-fed tCPA & asset upgrades reduce CPL.
  • Expand & Integrate: Non-branded SEO + TikTok/YouTube video funnel + remarketing = scale.

Key Risk

Policy disapprovals + privacy (HIPAA) constraints suppress active inventory & impression share. Must implement compliance-first creative framework.

🚨 Key Issue Clusters

1. Activation & Compliance

70%+ paused = suppressed scale. Policy disapprovals (weight loss / prescription phrasing) impede reach.

  • Centralize policy-safe copy blocks
  • Rebuild disapproved RSAs with medical-compliant terminology
  • Escalate chronic disapprovals via rep channel

2. Efficiency & Waste

Redundant & irrelevant queries inflate CPC and CPL. Lack of structured negatives & broad match strategy.

  • Daily search term triage (7-day rolling)
  • Negative taxonomy: Cost / Intent / Compliance tiers
  • Smart broad + audience layering (in-market aesthetics)

3. Creative & Experience

Poor RSA strength, no video assets in PMax, mobile LP speed deficits (≈10/100).

  • Video set: 6s hook / 15s testimonial / 30s explainer
  • LP sprint: Core Web Vitals + trust badges + privacy microcopy
  • Structured variant testing matrix (Headline × Offer × Proof)

4. Integration & Data

Offline conversions under-leveraged; SEO & Paid run in silos.

  • Weekly shared keyword report
  • CRM → GAds (Booked Consult, Procedure)
  • Organic intent buckets → Paid audience seeds
